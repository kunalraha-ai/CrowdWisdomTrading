# Agents Blueprint & Output Reference

This document maps the agent hierarchy, their associated skills, and provides sample output documents generated by each agent.

---

## 1. Marketing Manager Agent
The Marketing Manager Agent handles brand strategy, market positioning, competitor analysis, and organic content distribution.

### Skills
*   **`competitor_research.md`**: Gathers pricing, features, and social presence data for competitors and writes the Brand Positioning Guide.
*   **`social_post_repurposer.md`**: Converts generated video ad scripts into organic text posts for X and LinkedIn.

### Sample Outputs

#### Output A: Positioning & Competitor Matrix (`outputs/competitor_report.md`)
```markdown
# CrowdWisdomTrading Marketing Strategy & Competitor Report

## 1. CrowdWisdomTrading Positioning Guide
*   **Target Audience**: Retail Swing Traders, Crypto Investors, Part-time Traders.
*   **Core Value Propositions**: Noise Reduction, Collective Intelligence (5,000+ traders), Actionable Setups (Entry, Target, Stop).
*   **Tone of Voice**: Professional, realistic, direct.

## 2. Competitor Comparison Matrix
| Competitor | Focus | Pricing | Signals | Tone | Social Presence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **TradingView** | Charting & Social | Free - $60/mo | User setups | Community | Very High |
| **Benzinga Pro** | News & Alerts | $99 - $177/mo | News squawk | Urgent | High |
```

## 2. Ads Manager Agent
The Ads Manager Agent designs promotional campaigns, scrapes competitor ad libraries, extracts psychological angles, and drafts script materials.

### Skills
*   **`ad_scraping.md`**: Automatically searches Meta Ad Library via Apify for trading and investing niches.
*   **`pain_point_extraction.md`**: Extracts specific customer pain points, core promises, and persuasion techniques.
*   **`ad_script_writer.md`**: Writes video script copy designed for short-form (Reels/TikTok) and long-form (YouTube).
